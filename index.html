How it works

Organizing your grocery list just got a lot easier. With Quick List you can select the items you need to help you get in and out of the grocery store faster.

Easy as 1-2-3

1. Enter your items in the text field to the right

2. You new item will appear below

3. Use the to remove the item from your list

4. Use the to cross an item off your list

That's it. Easy-peasy.
